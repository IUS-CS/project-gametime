# Product Vision

## Overview
This website gives anyone a space to give their opinions on video games that they have played and share it for everyone to see.

## Core Purpose
The core purpose of the platform is to build a community of passionate users that get together and discuss the games they are interested in.

## Key Features
- **User Reviews & Opinions**  
  Users can write and share opinions on games they have played.

- **Social Following**  
  Users can follow other members to see their reviews and thoughts on games they care about.

- **Personalized Recommendations**  
  The website gives recommendations to users based on their favorite genres and passions

- **Game Rankings**  
  Games are ranked based on the scrutiny of the members.

- **Game Showcases**  
  Users can display:
  - Their favorite games  
  - Games currently in their backlog
